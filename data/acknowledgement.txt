
The 'char74k-digits-and-uppercase.csv' file in this directory was generated from a dataset created by Microsoft Research India.

It was introduced in this paper:

T. E. de Campos, B. R. Babu and M. Varma. Character recognition in natural images. In Proceedings of the International Conference on Computer Vision Theory and Applications (VISAPP), Lisbon, Portugal, February 2009.

The original dataset, 'EnglishHnd.tgz', can be downloaded from this website: http://www.ee.surrey.ac.uk/CVSSP/demos/chars74k/.
